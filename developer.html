Welcome to our learning hub for Basic Technology! Behind this vision is Arji Calistus Chukwujekwu, a passionate teacher, researcher, and lifelong learner who believes that every young mind can build, create, and innovate if given the right knowledge and guidance.

Mr. Arji is a graduate of Technical Education (Electrical/Electronics Option) from Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka. His thirst for knowledge and commitment to technical education led him to pursue a Master’s Degree in Technology at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka (UNN). Currently, he is advancing his expertise even further as he works toward a Doctorate Degree in Technology at UNN.

As someone who has walked the same learning path, he understands the challenges students face in understanding science and technology subjects. This is why he created this platform – to make Basic Technology fun, simple, and accessible to all junior secondary school students.

Through this website, students can explore exciting areas such as:

  • Automobile Technology – learning how cars and engines work.
  • Electrical and Electronics Technology – discovering the power of electricity and gadgets.
  • Woodwork Technology – working with wood and understanding carpentry.
  • Metalwork Technology – learning about metals, tools, and fabrication.
  • Building Construction – understanding how houses and structures are designed.

Mr. Arji believes that technology is not just about machines – it is about problem-solving, creativity, and preparing for the future. His dream is to inspire a new generation of Nigerian students who will become inventors, engineers, builders, and innovators that the world will celebrate.
